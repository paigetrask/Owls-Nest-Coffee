Who we are

Owl's Nest Coffee is Kara MacDonald.

Kara has lived in the Greater Seattle area her whole life, and has worked in the fast-paced coffee scene for 25 years.

She is dedicated to community-building as well as delivering an excellent cup of coffee, and places great importance on developing meaningful relationships with staff and patients.

The goal for Owl's Nest Coffee is to provide a place of peace for everyone at Providence Regional Cancer Partnership.

"Owl’s Nest Coffee was born out of the compassion and care my mother received when she was a patient here. Cancer touches practically all of us in one way or another, and I want to offer the same compassion and support to the people that find themselves here." -Kara
